About

I'm a physics engineer turned nanotechnologist with a hands-on command of the full semiconductor pipeline: design → fabrication → characterization. My master's work focused on process enhancement of amorphous-silicon single-photon avalanche diodes (SPADs), spanning photolithography, RIE and wet etching, mesa fabrication, and electro-optical characterization.

Earlier I co-founded and led a renewable-energy company, winning and delivering international projects in solar cells and PV modules. That blend of technical depth and commercial ownership is what I bring to a team — I can take a device from a simulation idea through cleanroom realization to a characterized, working result.

I'm currently seeking roles in semiconductor R&D / process engineering, photonics and THz/quantum devices, and research positions where rigorous experimental work matters.
